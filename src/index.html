About me

Left & right brain thinking

As a multidisciplinary creative professional, I bring a wealth of skills and experience to the table in the realms of art, web development, and entrepreneurship. With a solid foundation in classical painting, drawing, and sculpture, I earned a Masters of Fine Art degree that equipped me with exceptional creative problem-solving abilities.

I am highly proficient in full-stack web development, graphic design, and illustration, and offer a range of services in these fields. Whether it's designing a website, creating stunning graphics, or crafting compelling illustrations, I am committed to delivering top-notch results that exceed my clients' expectations.

My broad skillset and extensive experience make me a versatile and valuable asset for any project or team. So, if you're looking for a talented and professional creative partner, look no further.
